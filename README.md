# TrekTrack - Courier Management Website

TrekTrack is a robust, dynamic, and user-friendly Courier Management System built using PHP and MySQL. It is designed to automate and streamline the operations of logistics and courier service providers, allowing real-time tracking, efficient shipment handling, and user management.

---

## Features

### Shipment and Logistics Management
* **Real-time Tracking:** Customers can track their packages instantly using a unique tracking ID.
* **Order Creation:** Easy booking system for local and domestic shipments with automated weight and cost calculation.
* **Status Updates:** Update shipment statuses dynamically (e.g., Pending, In Transit, Out for Delivery, Delivered).

### User and Staff Roles
* **Admin Dashboard:** Central control panel to monitor total sales, active shipments, pending deliveries, and system users.
* **Rider/Delivery Personnel Panel:** Dedicated view for couriers to see assigned deliveries and update delivery statuses on the go.
* **Customer Portal:** Allows users to view their shipping history, manage profiles, and book new orders.

### Billing and Reporting
* **Invoice Generation:** Automatically generates downloadable/printable billing receipts for shipments.
* **Analytics:** Visual or tabular reports on daily, weekly, or monthly delivery performances and revenue.

---

* **Backend:** PHP (OOP architecture or Procedural depending on your project core)
